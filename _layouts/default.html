Engineering Final Project

Status: Complete | Platform: Wii

For our 4th year project at Carleton University, our task was to demonstrate the capabilities of connecting a WiiMote to an RC car via Bluetooth. This project brought together many technologies, including an Arduino board to communicate with the WiiMote, and a breadboard for connecting the wiring between the Arduino and remote control to the RC truck.
